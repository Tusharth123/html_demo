Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India

Narendra Modi, born on 17th September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat, is one of India's most influential political leaders. Rising from humble beginnings as a tea seller, he joined the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) in his youth and later became a full-time worker for the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). His strong organizational skills and grassroots connection helped him rise through the ranks, eventually becoming the Chief Minister of Gujarat in 2001, a position he held until 2014.

As Prime Minister since 2014, Modi has launched several major initiatives aimed at transforming India’s infrastructure and economy. Key programs like Digital India, Make in India, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, and Ayushman Bharat have focused on modernization, cleanliness, health, and digital connectivity. His foreign policy approach has also strengthened India’s global presence, forging closer ties with major powers while advocating for self-reliance through the "Atmanirbhar Bharat" initiative.

Modi is known for his charismatic oratory, disciplined lifestyle, and strong leadership style. While his tenure has been praised for development and decisive governance, it has also attracted criticism over issues like social polarization and press freedom. Despite differing opinions, Narendra Modi remains a central figure in contemporary Indian politics, shaping the nation’s trajectory in the 21st century.
